Privacy Policy for VV Upasthiti

  1. Information Collection: We collect student attendance data (name and attendance status) and the name of the staff member recording the attendance.
  2. Camera Usage: The app requires access to the device camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes for student check-in. No images or videos are stored or transmitted.
  3. Data Usage: Collected data is used exclusively for internal school management and attendance tracking via our secure Supabase backend.
  4. Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or rent any personal data to third parties.
  5. Data Security: Data is stored using industry-standard encryption provided by Supabase.
